Career aspirations

Where I'm headed

I want to practice architecture that takes climate seriously without treating comfort as a luxury — affordable housing and public buildings that stay cool without heavy air conditioning. After graduating, I'm aiming for a year of practical experience at a design-led firm before pursuing licensure, and eventually running a small studio focused on civic and community projects across West Africa.
